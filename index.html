Our Company

Bellows Gardens, Inc. is a fully licensed and insured Landscape/Garden Design and Maintenance company located in Southampton, NY. We aim to demonstrate the true beauty of nature with our various services that will transform your garden into a beautiful outdoor space. Our work turns your landscape into a breathtaking scene while keeping your needs in mind. Our maintenance work ensures your garden remains beautiful and ensures your plants last to their fullest potential.

Our family owned business has been around for over a hundred years! Starting in 1922 as Bellows Brothers Nursery, the business was founded in Southampton where the original barn still stands today.
