Trimming

❮ ❯

Press the arrows to see a picture!

The trimming we do has been recognized all over Southampton and the East-end of Long Island. The trimming specialists begin by designing custom-made support/guideline structures for a specific shrub or hedge. These structures are used to create a perfect first cut. Our specialists are then able to replicate the first cut using their free-handing skills until the hedge or shrubs grow too big. This process helps creates a beautiful and unique piece for your garden or the beautification of your means of privacy.
